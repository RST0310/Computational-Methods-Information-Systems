# AI Cricket Narrator: Bridging the Gap between Human and AI Commentary for Enhanced Viewer Experience
Spring Semester Course UNT, Computational Methods for Information Systems

Introducing AI Cricket Narrator, a groundbreaking project at the intersection of sports and technology! Leveraging advancements in natural language processing and machine learning, we're bridging the gap between human and AI commentary for an enhanced viewer experience in cricket matches. Our goal is to automate the process of generating insightful commentary in real-time, revolutionizing how fans engage with the game
